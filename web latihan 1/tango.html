Tango

"This is how to dance the Tango! Feel the blood rise to your face with every beat; While an arm winds like a snake around your waist that is about to break. That is how to dance the Tango!" -Elizardo Martinez Vilas

"The Tango is man and woman in search of each other. It is the search for embrace, a way to be together, when the man feels like a man and the woman feels like a woman, without machismo. The music arouses and torments, the dance is the coupling of two people, defenseless against the world and powerless to change things." - Juan Carlos Copes
